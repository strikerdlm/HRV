- not be a current colleague or co-author within the last 3 years,
- not be on the *Applied Ergonomics* editorial board,
- represent diverse geography (do not propose only Latin-American or only US/EU candidates),
- be reachable at an **institutional** email (not ResearchGate / Gmail / Hotmail).
